Expertise lives below the level of language.

The decisions your best people make on instinct. The workarounds they built because the system couldn't keep up. The knowledge that holds everything together and lives in exactly one person's head.

LUMA makes it visible.

Analytical light for how your business actually operates.

A single recorded conversation with the person who runs your business. Sixty minutes. No preparation required beyond showing up ready to talk honestly about how things actually work — not the polished version, the real one.

That conversation is processed through the Prism — a proprietary analytical engine that examines what you said through several tuned lenses, each calibrated to a different dimension of your operations. How you make decisions. Where communication breaks down. What your team actually does versus what you think they do. Where your history is still shaping your present. Where your vision and your direction of travel have quietly diverged.

What comes back is not a summary. It's a map of territory you've been walking through blind. A multi-resolution document where you see the strategic picture and your team sees operational detail — the same analysis, self-selecting depth. Every finding grounded in your own words.
